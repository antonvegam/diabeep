Welcome to CardioBeep

At CardioBeep, we bring you an innovative solution to predict cardiovascular disease risk in diabetic patients using advanced wearable technology and AI-driven analysis.

Join us in transforming healthcare with proactive monitoring and timely intervention!

Key Features

Continuous Monitoring

Wearable devices continuously track heart rate, ECG, blood pressure, and other vital signs.

Real-Time Data Analysis

Advanced AI algorithms analyze data in real-time to detect potential cardiovascular risks.

Alerts and Notifications

Immediate alerts sent to healthcare providers and patients for timely intervention.

Integration with Medical Records

Seamlessly integrates with existing electronic medical records (EMRs) for comprehensive health insights.

Personalized Recommendations

Provides tailored health recommendations based on continuous data and patient history.

Patient App

User-friendly mobile app for patients to monitor their health and receive personalized advice.

How CardioBeep Works

Data Collection

Wearable devices collect continuous biometric data, including heart rate, ECG, and blood pressure.

Data Transmission

Data is securely transmitted to the cloud-based system via APIs and VPN.

Data Analysis

The cloud-based AI analyzes the data to predict cardiovascular risk, integrating additional data like lab results and stress levels when available.

Risk Assessment

The system generates a risk score and alerts healthcare providers and patients of potential issues.

Actionable Insights

Personalized health recommendations are provided to patients and healthcare providers for proactive management.
